About TerraSentinel

TerraSentinel is a BSc project by Ergi Hokja for disaster risk management using AI and real-time data. The system aims to predict earthquakes, floods, and wildfires, providing early warnings and interactive risk monitoring.

Data sources: NASA, USGS, NOAA, and more.

For project details, contact: ergi.hokja@study.beds.ac.uk
